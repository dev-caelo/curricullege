Why are scholarships worth applying for?

As the cost of higher education increases, the importance of scholarships for upcoming students has only grown greater. By lessening the amount of debt students take-on during college, scholarships have grown to become a necessity for most college students.

T Y P E S

Academic

An academic scholarship is based on educational accomplishments and/or personal achievement. To receive this, one must work on their stats, such as GPA and SAT score

Community Service

Given to students who show that they can give back to their community in different forms, through either extensive and meaningful volunteering or developing influential programs

Personal Background

These are awarded to those that are a part of a generally underrepresented group, including ethnic minorities and members of the LGBTQ+ community.

Financial Need

Financial need scholarships are intended to aid students living in a lower income household.

R E S O U R C E S

Collegevine

Collegevine is a great source for guidance through the college application process, featuring an admissions chance calculator and entrance essay support.

Prepscholar

Prepscholar focuses on providing free SAT and ACT prep to help students ensure optimal performance on their college entrance exams. They also give insightful tips on how to shape your applications from early highschool.

BuildYourFuture

Google's very own scholarship program. It hosts of a number of opportunities for ethnic minorities, scholarships for women aspiring to join the tech industry, and events that can be listed on one's college resume!

Fastweb

Fastweb offers free scholarship search services in order to aid students in their efforts to reduce the cost of their secondary education.
