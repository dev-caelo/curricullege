what we do

Curricullege compiles a compacted form of various college-related resources, as well as advice for high school students. Students will be able to access our website to receive support and guidance for competitive colleges.

Welcome to your source for all things college.

why we do it

Our mission is to provide guidance and resources that students can use to aid them in college apps. We are students, just like you, and are passionate about helping you make your eventual transition from high school to college as seamless and stress-free as possible.

who we do it for

We hope to reach any student who is in search of guidance about academics and the college admissions process. We also provide resources for students who would like to strengthen time management, finding balance, and coping with the intensity of the academic pressures of high school and college.
