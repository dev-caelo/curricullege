Why do they matter?

Participating in academic competitions show colleges that you are willing to continue learning outside of school and genuinely desire to continue growing as a student. They help showcase your knowledge and passion for a specific subject, and will bring this energy to the colleges you apply to. Below are links to competitions you may be interested in.

M A T H
